# Human Evaluation & Pricing

## Human Evaluation

To enable fast, rigorous, and consistent leaderboard evaluation across submissions, we provide outsourced human evaluation through our official evaluation partner, [Careerflow.ai](https://careerflow.ai/human-data).

All evaluations follow a standardized multi-stage review process, including:

- Multiple independent annotators
- Human task attempts
- Full trajectory review
- QA verification and final review

This process helps reduce reviewer bias and ensures consistent evaluation quality across different submissions.

For the full evaluation protocol, label definitions, criteria, edge cases, and partner details, see [DETAILED_HUMAN_EVALUATION_PROCESS.md](./DETAILED_HUMAN_EVALUATION_PROCESS.md).
